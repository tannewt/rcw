Section 1 ¶

Section 2 ¶

  1. This chapter does not apply to the use of reusable packing materials that are part of a packing material sharing and reuse program.

  2. For purposes of this section, "packing material sharing and reuse program" and "reusable packing materials" have the same meanings as in RCW 82.04.050(4)(c).

Section 3 ¶

This act takes effect August 1, 2023.
