# HB 2138 - Fuel licensees tax rate
**Primary Sponsor:** [Jim Walsh](/person/leg/jim.walsh.md)

*Status: First reading, referred to Transportation (Not Officially read and referred until adoption of Introduction report).* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=2138&Year=2021)

Reducing the cumulative tax rate upon fuel licensees to half the amount that is imposed in 2022.
