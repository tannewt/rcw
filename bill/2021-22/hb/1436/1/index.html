Section 1 ¶

This section adds a new section to an existing chapter 43.06. Here is the modified chapter for context.

  1. The definitions in this subsection apply throughout this section unless the context clearly requires otherwise.

    1. "COVID-19" means a respiratory disease caused by the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2).

    2. "COVID-19 pandemic emergency" means the state of emergency relating to the outbreak and spread of COVID-19 declared in the governor's proclamation 20-05 on February 29, 2020, and any renewals thereof.

    3. "COVID-19 restrictive orders" means the following orders of the governor related to the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, and any renewals thereof: 20-15, 20-20, 20-23, 20-28, 20-30, 20-31, 20-32, 20-36, 20-41, 20-43, 20-44, 20-45, 20-48, 20-49, 20-51, 20-52, 20-56, 20-59, 20-64, 20-65, 20-66, 20-69, and 20-74.

  2. Until 12 months after the cessation or withdrawal of any COVID-19 restrictive order and the COVID-19 pandemic emergency, the following authorizations are temporarily not required of any residential construction, commercial construction, industrial construction, industrial facility operations, or activities undertaken by a public port:

    1. A permit issued under chapter 90.48 RCW;

    2. A permit issued under chapter 70A.15 RCW;

    3. A permit issued under chapter 77.55 RCW; and

    4. Any project permit as defined in RCW 36.70B.020.
