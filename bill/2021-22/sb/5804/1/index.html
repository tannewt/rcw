Section 1 ¶

This section adds a new section to an existing chapter 70.54. Here is the modified chapter for context.

  1. A person may not conduct gain-of-function research in the state.

  2. A person who violates this section is subject to a civil penalty not to exceed $10,000 for each violation, as determined by the court.

  3. [Empty]

    1. The attorney general may receive, investigate, and prosecute complaints against alleged violators of this section.

    2. The attorney general may institute and conduct an action in the name of the state of Washington for any of the following:

      1. An injunction in any court of this state for injunctive relief to restrain a person from continuing any activity that violates this section;

      2. The assessment and recovery of civil penalties provided in subsection (2) of this section.

    3. The attorney general must be reimbursed through civil penalties collected under this section for the costs incurred in providing the services described in this subsection. Any remaining funds must be deposited in the state general fund.

  4. For the purposes of this section, "gain-of-function research" means experimentation that aims or is expected to increase the transmissibility or virulence, or both, of a pathogen.
