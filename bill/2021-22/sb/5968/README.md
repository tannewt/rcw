# SB 5968 - Salmon populations/effect

*Status: First reading, referred to Agriculture, Water, Natural Resources & Parks.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5968&Year=2021)

Requiring the department of fish and wildlife to track the effect of certain specified activities on salmon populations.
