# HB 1026 - LTSS trust spousal benefits

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2025-26/Pdf/Bills/House%20Bills/1026.pdf)

## Section 1
1. A qualified individual may elect to transfer any or all of the qualified individual's available benefit units to the qualified individual's spouse if the receiving spouse:

    a. [Empty]

        i. Does not meet the criteria to be a qualified individual under RCW 50B.04.050; and

        ii. Following an evaluation, is determined to meet the functional criteria necessary to become an eligible beneficiary as established in RCW 50B.04.060; or

    b. [Empty]

        i. Has met the criteria to be a qualified individual under RCW 50B.04.050;

        ii. Following an evaluation, is determined to meet the functional criteria necessary to become an eligible beneficiary as established in RCW 50B.04.060; and

        iii. Has exhausted the benefit units available to the receiving spouse under the program.

2. A qualified individual who transfers benefit units to a spouse shall have the amount of transferred units deducted from the amount of benefit units available to the qualified individual who makes the transfer.
