# SB 5086 - Voter eligibility/felony
**Primary Sponsor:** [Patty Kuderer](/person/leg/patty.kuderer.md)

*Status: By resolution, reintroduced and retained in present status.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5086&Year=2021)

Restoring voter eligibility for all persons convicted of a felony offense who are not in total confinement under the jurisdiction of the department of corrections.
