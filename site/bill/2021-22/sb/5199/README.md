# SB 5199 - Tribal fishing rights
**Primary Sponsor:** [Kevin Van De Wege](/person/leg/kevin.vandewege.md)

*Status: By resolution, reintroduced and retained in present status.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5199&Year=2021)

Recognizing judicially affirmed and treaty-reserved fishing rights and promoting state-tribal cooperative agreements in the management of salmon, trout, and steelhead resources.
