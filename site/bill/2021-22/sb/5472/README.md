# SB 5472 - Utility customers/COVID-19
**Primary Sponsor:** [Kevin Van De Wege](/person/leg/kevin.vandewege.md)

*Status: By resolution, reintroduced and retained in present status.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5472&Year=2021)

Providing support for utility customers impacted by COVID-19 through payment plans, including partial forgiveness of arrearages and a credit against the public utility tax.
