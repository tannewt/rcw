# Substitute Senate Bill 5736 as Recommended by Subcommittee on Behavioral Health

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2021-22/Pdf/Bills/Senate%20Bills/5736-S.pdf)
## Section 2
The health care authority shall take the steps necessary to add coverage for partial hospitalization and intensive outpatient services for persons under 21 years of age to the medicaid state plan by January 1, 2024.
