# Substitute Senate Bill 5912 as Recommended by Health & Long Term Care

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2021-22/Pdf/Bills/Senate%20Bills/5912-S.pdf)
## Section 1
> This section adds a new section to an existing chapter [74.09](/rcw/74_public_assistance/74.09_medical_care.md). Here is the [modified chapter](rcw/74_public_assistance/74.09_medical_care.md) for context.

Beginning July 1, 2022, the authority shall ensure the periodicity schedules for the early and periodic screening, diagnosis, and treatment benefit provided under this chapter are aligned with the bright futures guidelines of the American academy of pediatrics or a comparable standard.
