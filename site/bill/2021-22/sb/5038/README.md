# SB 5038 - Open carry of weapons
**Primary Sponsor:** [Patty Kuderer](/person/leg/patty.kuderer.md)

*Status: Effective date 5/12/2021.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5038&Year=2021)

Prohibiting the open carry of certain weapons at public permitted demonstrations and the state capitol.
