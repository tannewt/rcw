# House Bill 1253

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2021-22/Pdf/Bills/House%20Bills/1253.pdf)
## Section 4
> This section adds a new section to an existing chapter [46.04](/rcw/46_motor_vehicles/46.04_definitions.md). Here is the [modified chapter](rcw/46_motor_vehicles/46.04_definitions.md) for context.

"Working forests license plates" means special license plates issued under RCW 46.18.200 that display images of a fish and a forest with text supporting working forests.
