# House Bill 1420

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2021-22/Pdf/Bills/House%20Bills/1420.pdf)
## Section 1
> This section adds a new section to an existing chapter [70.54](/rcw/70_public_health_and_safety/70.054_miscellaneous_health_and_safety_provisions.md). Here is the [modified chapter](rcw/70_public_health_and_safety/70.054_miscellaneous_health_and_safety_provisions.md) for context.

1. Any criteria used by the state to allocate vaccine doses for the COVID-19 virus must include the following persons in the category of critical workers given first priority for receipt of the vaccine:

    a. Public and private school teachers;

    b. Classified on-site public school staff; and

    c. Private school employees who are the equivalent of classified on-site public school staff.

2. This section supersedes any allocation criteria issued by the state prior to the effective date of this section.
