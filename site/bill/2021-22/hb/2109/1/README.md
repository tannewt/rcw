# House Bill 2109

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2021-22/Pdf/Bills/House%20Bills/2109.pdf)
## Section 1
> This section modifies existing section [44.04.010](/rcw/44_state_government—legislative/44.04_general_provisions.md). Here is the [modified chapter](rcw/44_state_government—legislative/44.04_general_provisions.md) for context.

Regular sessions of the legislature shall be held annually, commencing on the second Monday of January. **During each odd-numbered year, the regular session shall not be more than 60 consecutive days. During each even-numbered year, the regular session shall not be more than one day.**
