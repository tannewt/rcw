HB 2428 - Sales and use tax sharing ¶

Source

Section 1 ¶

  1. Cities and towns may enter into an agreement under this chapter to share a portion of general purpose sales and use tax revenue collected under RCW 82.14.030.

  2. Any agreement to share such revenue must specify, in addition to the conditions required to be included under RCW 39.34.030, the following:

    1. If the agreement applies only to sales and use tax revenue collected in a certain area or areas, then the area or areas in which it is applicable;

    2. The amount or proportion of the sales and use tax revenue that is to be shared; and

    3. The precise mechanism or method that will be used to share the revenue.

  3. This section shall not be construed to diminish or reduce the authority of local governments to enter into agreements under this chapter or as otherwise allowed by law.

Section 2 ¶

  1. The rate of sales and use tax imposed by a city under RCW 82.14.030 (1) and (2) may be altered pursuant to a government service agreement as provided in RCW 36.115.040 and 36.115.050.

  2. Revenue from a sales and use tax imposed by a city under RCW 82.14.030 (1) and (2) may be shared with another city as part of an agreement entered into pursuant to section 1 of this act.
